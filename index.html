Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about bird nets for balconies? We’ve got you covered!

What is a bird net and why do I need it for my balcony?

Bird nets are protective mesh installations that prevent birds like pigeons from entering your balcony, keeping it clean and safe. They also help avoid bird droppings, nests, and potential health hazards.

Are bird nets safe for birds? Will it harm them?

Absolutely safe! Bird nets are designed to block entry without harming birds. They act as a barrier, guiding birds away without trapping or injuring them.

How durable are these nets? Will they survive rains and sun?

Yes, we use UV-stabilized, weatherproof materials that can withstand heavy rains, harsh sunlight, and strong winds. Our premium nets come with multi-year warranties for durability.

How long does installation take for a standard balcony?

Usually, a standard residential balcony net installation takes about 1-2 hours, depending on size and complexity. For larger or custom projects, it might take slightly longer.

Do you provide warranty and maintenance services?

Yes, we offer up to 3 years of warranty on premium nets. Maintenance services are available on request, ensuring your nets stay in top condition.
