Bird Spikes — Clean, Humane Bird Control

Say goodbye to bird poop, noise, and nests. Our Bird Spikes prevent pigeons and other birds from landing on ledges, pipes, AC units, and rooftops—without hurting them.

Block Birds the Smart & Safe Way

Rayan Safety Nets offers high-quality Bird Spikes made from durable polycarbonate or stainless steel that act as a physical barrier to keep birds away. They're discreet, weather-resistant, and totally harmless—just an effective way to stop unwanted perching and nesting.

  • Prevents bird droppings, nesting, and property damage.
  • Safe for birds — no electricity, no chemicals, no harm.
  • Ideal for building ledges, AC units, pipes, signboards & rooftops.
  • Easy to install and maintenance-free.

Whether you're dealing with pigeons on your balcony or keeping rooftops clear in commercial spaces, our bird spike systems offer long-lasting, non-invasive control. The sleek, low-visibility design blends right in with your building’s aesthetics.

Keep your property clean and peaceful with Rayan Safety Nets' Bird Spikes—because bird problems need smart solutions, not harsh ones.
