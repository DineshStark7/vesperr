Block the heat, not the vibe. Our Shade Nets are designed to reduce sunlight intensity while maintaining airflow—perfect for farms, gardens, balconies, and outdoor workspaces.

Reliable Shade Nets for Homes, Farms & Industries

Too much sun can damage crops, heat up spaces, and wear down your energy. Rayan Safety Nets provides premium-grade Shade Nets that offer up to 90% UV protection—keeping your space cooler, safer, and more efficient.

  • Made from HDPE with UV stabilization for long-lasting durability.
  • Available in various densities (35% to 90%) based on shade requirement.
  • Perfect for agriculture, nurseries, terraces, car parking, and construction sites.
  • Lightweight, breathable, and easy to install or relocate.

We offer installation for both temporary and permanent setups, with customized sizes to suit your space. Whether you’re trying to boost crop yield or just chill under the shade, our nets deliver both function and form.
